How to Apply

We're delighted that you're considering Brainfield International Schools for your child's education. Our admission process is designed to help us get to know your child and ensure our school is the right fit for their educational journey.

1

Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents

2

Assessment

Your child will take our entrance assessment exam

3

Decision

Receive admission decision within 2 weeks of assessment

Admission Requirements

To complete your application, please prepare the following documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original and copy)
  • Previous school records/transcripts
  • Recommendation letters (2)
  • Medical records and immunization history
  • Passport-sized photographs (4)
  • Parent/guardian identification documents
  • Application fee receipt

Please note that additional documents may be required for international students or specific grade levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our admission process

What is the application deadline?

Our main application deadline is May 31st for the following academic year. However, we accept applications on a rolling basis throughout the year if spaces are available.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, Brainfield School offers need-based financial aid to qualified students. Applications for financial aid are processed separately from admission applications.

What are the age requirements for kindergarten?

Children entering kindergarten must be 3 years old by September 1st of the academic year they are applying for.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for students entering grades 6-12. Scholarship applications are due by March 15th.

Can we schedule a campus tour?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective families to visit our campus. You can schedule a tour by calling our admissions office or using the online visit request form.
