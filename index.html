About Brainfield International Schools

Discover what makes our institution a leader in education

Providing Quality Education Since 2012

Brainfield School was established with a vision to create an educational establishment that nurtures young minds and prepares them for the challenges of the future. For over 10 years, we have been committed to academic excellence and holistic development.

Our campuses, with state-of-the-art facilities, includes modern classrooms, science and computer labs, a well-stocked library, and extensive sports facilities.

We take pride in our diverse student community and our dedicated faculty who are experts in their respective fields and passionate about teaching.
